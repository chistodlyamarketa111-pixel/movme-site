Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Introduction

Movme ("we", "our", "the app") is a meditation application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Information We Collect

  • Account data: When you sign up, we collect your email address, display name, and age range to personalize your experience.
  • Authentication data: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email from Google. We do not access your Google password.
  • Usage data: We store your meditation preferences and practice history to provide personalized recommendations.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and personalize the meditation experience
  • To maintain your account and session
  • To improve the app

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform with encryption at rest and in transit.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Database and authentication
  • Google Sign-In: Optional authentication method

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by law.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.
